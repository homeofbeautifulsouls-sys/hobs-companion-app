1. What HOBS Companion is

HOBS Companion is a mental health support and self-care tool provided by Home of Beautiful Souls Foundation. It offers mood tracking, journaling, task management, guided screening tools, grounding exercises, and the ability to book sessions with therapists who work with HOBS.

HOBS Companion is not a medical device, a diagnostic tool, or an emergency service. It does not replace professional mental health care. If you are in crisis or immediate danger, call 112 or iCall at 9152987821 (Mon–Sat, 10am–8pm) right away.

5. Booking sessions with therapists

  • Therapists available through the App are independent professionals working with HOBS; booking a session creates a professional relationship between you and that therapist, governed by their own professional and ethical standards in addition to these terms.
  • Payment for sessions is handled via the QR-code flow in the App and confirmed manually by our team.
  • Cancellations:
    • If you cancel a session with more than 24 hours' notice, the full amount you paid carries forward and is applied to your next session — no charge.
    • If you cancel with less than 24 hours' notice, a 50% cancellation charge applies; the remaining 50% carries forward to your next session. This 50% rate applies once per calendar month — if you cancel late again within the same month, that cancellation is charged at 100% (no carry-forward).
    • Refunds are not offered — amounts are carried forward toward future sessions rather than returned.
  • Credits: You can earn credits by completing tasks in the App, at ₹1 per task, with no daily limit, capped at ₹300 per calendar month. To redeem a given month's earned credits, you need to have completed at least 4 booked therapy sessions in that same week, each week of that month. If you don't meet that bar in a given month, that month's credits lapse entirely (they do not carry over or roll forward). If you do meet it, that month's credits become a voucher usable during the following month, applied automatically via the QR code at checkout.
  • HOBS facilitates the booking and payment process but the therapeutic relationship and clinical judgment are the therapist's responsibility.
