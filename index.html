Informed Consent — Home of Beautiful Souls Foundation

This is a real therapy consent agreement, not just app terms. It covers tele-consultation with our Clinical Team (Therapist, Peer Caregiver, and Psychiatrist).

Roles

Therapist: direct tele-psychotherapy, session notes, assessments, treatment recommendations. Peer Caregiver: non-clinical emotional/social support only — never diagnosis, treatment planning, or independent crisis intervention. Psychiatrist: medical oversight and pharmacological treatment where needed, on referral from your Therapist.

Confidentiality

Sessions are confidential and not recorded. Details are shared only if legally mandated, or with your specific consent for supervision/training purposes. Recording or sharing session content yourself is a serious breach and may end services.

Booking & Cancellation

Sessions are booked and confirmed through the app. Cancelling more than 24 hours ahead carries no charge — the amount carries forward. Cancelling within 24 hours carries a 50% charge (once per calendar month); a second late cancellation in the same month is charged in full.

Emergencies & Limits

This app is not an emergency service. In a genuine emergency, contact local emergency services or a crisis line directly — iCall: 9152987821 (Mon–Sat, 10am–8pm), or 112. If you're at risk of harming yourself or someone else, we may need to inform someone who can help protect you, including a listed emergency contact, even without your separate consent for that specific disclosure.

Responsibility

You understand the Clinical Team will use their professional judgment, and you agree not to hold them or HOBS responsible for adverse outcomes (lack of improvement, deterioration, or risk of harm) that occur during consultation — though you may still be advised to seek emergency or in-person care when needed.

No Suicide Agreement

As part of this consultation, you agree that if you ever want to hurt or kill yourself, you will reach out to one of the emergency contacts you list below, or call them directly, before acting on that urge — and that you'll continue engaging with the Clinical Team rather than facing it alone.

Your Data

How we handle everything you share is covered in full in our Privacy Policy, in line with India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023.
